What we do

Nursing care built around your family

Every Avacago visit is led by a registered nurse and planned around the patient — not a generic checklist. Here's exactly what each type of care includes.

01

Hygiene Care

Full head-to-foot personal care for patients who can't manage bathing and grooming on their own, done with dignity and privacy.

Bed bathsSkin checksDaily care
  • Head washing
  • Eye care
  • Mouth care
  • Back care
  • Perineal care
  • Foot care
  • Skin care & monitoring
02

Clinical Care

Hospital-level clinical procedures performed safely at home by a registered nurse, so patients can recover in familiar surroundings.

IV therapyWound careTube feeding
  • Blood drawing
  • IV cannulation & fluids
  • IV antibiotics
  • Tracheostomy care
  • Wound care
  • Stoma care
  • PEG feeding
  • Nasogastric feeding
  • Urinary catheterization
03

Palliative & Psychosocial Care

Comfort-focused nursing for patients with serious illness, plus steady, compassionate support for those in psychological or emotional distress.

Comfort careCounsellingFamily support
  • Palliative nursing care
  • Pain & symptom monitoring
  • Support for psychological illness
  • Care during psychosocial crisis
  • One-on-one counselling
  • Support for family caregivers
04

Mobility & Paralysis Support

Dedicated nursing support for patients who are bedbound, partially paralysed, or find it difficult to move on their own.

RepositioningPressure careDaily support
  • Safe repositioning & transfers
  • Pressure sore prevention
  • Assisted mobility exercises
  • Full paralysis nursing care
Our founders

Why we started Avacago

"We built Avacago on a simple belief — that technology can strengthen Sri Lanka's healthcare system and bring quality nursing care to every home, not just the hospitals that are easiest to reach. By looking at home nursing through fresh, innovative ideas, we're building a care system that meets people where they are."
[ Randew Wisumperuma ]
Co-Founder & CEO, Avacago
"We started Avacago because too many families in Sri Lanka were struggling to get a patient to a clinic just for basic care. We wanted to bring the nurse to them instead — with the same standard of care, at their door."
[ DMR Chandima Dissanayke ]
Co-Founder, COO & Head of Nursing, Avacago
